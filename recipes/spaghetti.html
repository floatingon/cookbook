Mom's Spaghetti

Description

Knees weak, arms are heavy. This is Mom's Spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (lean)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, sliced (thin)
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 6-8 cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 2 cans diced tomato with Italian herbs
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 package Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Idaho Gold Dust

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Saute garlic, onions, mushrooms (in juices from ground beef - add a bit of olive oil if needed)
  3. Combine browned ground beef, garlic, onions, mushrooms with all other ingredients in crock pot and cook on low to medium for several hours.
  4. Serve over cooked spaghetti noodles and top with parmesan cheese :)
