Armenian Rice

Description

Armenian rice -- a Lyon family classic! For special rice, add a little white wine or sherry to uncooked rice and butter in pan. Add liquid and cook as usual.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 bacon slices, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups long grain white rice, uncooked
  • 3 tablespoons soysauce
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 1 4oz can mushrooms, drained
  • 1 8oz package almonds, slivered
  • 2 cups beef consomme, undiluted
  • 2 cups beef bouillon, undiluted

Steps

  1. In a medium skillet, saute bacon and onion.
  2. Add butter and heat just to melt butter.
  3. In a 3 to 4-quart casserole, mix bacon/onion mixture with remaining ingredients.
  4. Bake covered at 300°F (150°C) for 2 hours or until rice is tender.
