Artichoke Dip

Description

This is quick and easy and always a big hit.

Ingredients

  1. 2 8-ounce cans artichoke hearts in water, drained and roughly cut
  2. 2 4-ounce can chopped green chiles
  3. 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  4. 1 cup mayonnaise
  5. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and put in 1-quart baking dish
  2. Bake uncovered at 350Â° for 20 minutes
  3. Serve with tortilla chips
